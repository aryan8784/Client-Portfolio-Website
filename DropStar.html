The Client’s Vision:

The client had developed a hardware tool for gamers to overlay audio clips onto live streams and online multiplayer games. They tasked us with developing the software to operate the system and communicate with the hardware through the Internet of Things (IoT).

The Problem:

The client needed a way for gamers to easily search, trim, and save clips from their favorite YouTube videos to use as audio overlays on their live streams and multiplayer games. Additionally, the hardware and software needed to seamlessly communicate with each other with zero latency.

The Solution:

Our unique 7-step process for building new products – visioning, scoping, designing, developing, testing, launching, and iterating – was used to bring the client’s vision to life. Our design team crafted premium high-fidelity designs that kept to the client’s core thesis of simplicity. Our development team built a top-tier product that seamlessly integrated several APIs, including proprietary APIs, to enable users to search, trim, and save their favorite clips. Furthermore, our development team architected an intelligent algorithm to produce a recommendation engine based on what the user-base was currently interested in. This algorithm was designed to solve user’s problems effectively and generate revenue.

Our Outcome:

The end-product delivered significant value for the client’s end-users by providing a seamless and easy way to search, trim and save clips from their favorite YouTube videos to use as audio overlays on their live streams and multiplayer games. Furthermore, the software and hardware communicated seamlessly with zero latency. Our collaboration with the client resulted in a top-tier product that exceeded their expectations. The mobile app was able to generate revenue and had a viral growth of users due to the product’s ability to solve the user’s problems effectively.
