The need

As a leading mobile app development company, We approached by a global eCommerce fitness brand, to develop a mobile app to increase the lifetime value of their customers. The client wanted to deliver personalised home workout programs and nutritional meal plans through the app.

Our approach

To achieve this goal, We implemented the following steps:

User-Centric Design:

The Mobeen Design Team used the latest user-centric design methods to create UI/UX designs from scratch. This ensured that the app would be intuitive and easy to use for the client’s customers.

Agile Development:

In just eight weeks, our Mobile App Development Team engineered a professional full-stack iOS and Android app using advanced video processing technologies. We used best-in-class agile methodologies to ensure that the app was delivered on time and to the highest standards.

Video Processing Technologies:

The mobile app had terabytes of video to process, and our team designed the backend architecture and frontend framework to deliver 4K video streaming with instantaneous buffering speed. This was crucial for delivering a seamless user experience for the client’s customers.

Global Launch and Seamless Scaling:

When the mobile app launched globally, it scaled seamlessly alongside the client’s existing product range. This was important for maintaining a consistent user experience across all platforms.

Recurring In-App Purchases:

Mobeen implemented recurring in-app purchases, which helped to increase the lifetime value of the client’s customers. This was a key factor in achieving the client’s goal of growing revenue and customer satisfaction.

The outcome

Thanks to Mobeen expertise in mobile app development, the app launched globally and scaled seamlessly alongside The client’s existing product range. Mobeen implementation of recurring in-app purchases also helped to increase the lifetime value of The client’s customers.
