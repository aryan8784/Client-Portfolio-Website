The Vision:

The client, a social investing platform, approached me with a vision to create a mobile app that would revolutionize the way people invest. They wanted to create an app that would allow users to share their investment strategies and insights with one another, and to make it easy for them to track and manage their investments. They also wanted the app to generate revenue through in-app purchases and advertising.

The Problem:

The problem that the client faced was that existing social investing platforms were not user-friendly and did not have the features that users needed to effectively track and manage their investments. Additionally, there was no easy way for users to share their investment strategies and insights with one another.

How the Product Solves the Problem :

Our unique 7-step process for building new products was the perfect solution for the client’s needs. We started by conducting a thorough visioning process to fully understand the client’s goals and objectives. We then moved on to the scoping phase, where we worked with the client to define the features and functionality that the app would need to have.
Next, we moved on to the designing phase, where we created wireframes and mockups of the app’s interface. During the development phase, we used the latest technologies and frameworks to build a high-performance, secure and scalable app.
Once the app was developed, we moved on to the testing phase, where we thoroughly tested the app to ensure that it met all of the client’s requirements and was free of bugs. We then launched the app, and began the iterating phase, where we continued to work with the client to add new features, fix bugs and improve the app’s performance.

Our Outcome:

The final product was a highly-functional and user-friendly mobile app that met all of the client’s needs. The app had a wide range of features that made it easy for users to track and manage their investments, and it also had a social aspect that allowed users to share their investment strategies and insights with one another. Additionally, the app generated revenue through in-app purchases and advertising, and it experienced viral growth of users due to its unique features and user-friendly interface.
Through our 7-step process, the client benefitted from working with an expert mobile app development company who understood their vision and could build a successful mobile app quickly. Furthermore, our team’s clear communication with the client throughout the entire process ensured that the client was well-informed of the progress and felt heard in the decision-making process. Overall, the client was extremely satisfied with the outcome and the app was a huge success.
