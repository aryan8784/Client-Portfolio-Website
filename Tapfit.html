The Client’s Vision:

The client, a global eCommerce brand that promotes a fun, fit, and healthy lifestyle through tap dance, wanted to enhance their customers’ experience with a highly engaging mobile app that would increase their lifetime value.

The Problem:

The client needed a mobile app that would provide an immersive experience for their customers, allowing them to interact with the brand in a more meaningful way and boosting the lifetime value of their business.

The Solution:

Mobeen, an expert mobile app development company in London, employed its unique 7-step process for building new products, including visioning, scoping, designing, developing, testing, launching, and iterating, to bring the client’s vision to life. We collaborated closely with the client to comprehend their specific needs and goals for the app.
Our design team created a visually stunning and user-friendly interface that seamlessly integrated the client’s existing eCommerce platform. The app was designed to make it easy for users to browse and purchase products, as well as access exclusive content such as instructional videos and dance routines.
Our development team built a robust and scalable app that could handle high traffic and large amounts of data, while also integrating features such as push notifications, social media sharing, and a personalized feed, to increase engagement and retention.

The Mobeen Outcome:

The end product exceeded the client’s expectations and provided an immersive experience for their customers. The app successfully increased customer engagement and retention, resulting in a significant increase in lifetime value for the client’s business.
