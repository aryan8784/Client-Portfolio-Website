The Client’s Vision:

The client, a fintech company, approached me with a mission to simplify the process of spending, investing, and interacting with Africa. They wanted to provide a one-stop-shop for these activities, with a focus on convenience and access to Africa. They envisioned a multi-currency wallet that doubles as a local currency wallet, instant transfers, protection for medical bills, and the ability to purchase essentials for loved ones in Africa. They also planned to offer wealth building opportunities through partnerships and access to the African capital and goods market.

The problem that the product solves:

Currently, spending, investing, and interacting with Africa can be a difficult and time-consuming process, requiring a lot of paperwork and dealing with bureaucracy. Additionally, many people are hesitant to enter the African market due to concerns about security and reliability.

How the product solves the problem:

To solve these challenges, My team of experts employed a unique 7-step process, including visioning, scoping, designing, developing, testing, launching and iterating. Our team created a user-friendly mobile app that allows users to open an account in less than 2 minutes and start transacting within minutes.
The app includes high-level security features and simple transfer methods with no hidden costs. It also has coverage across Sub-Saharan Africa and multi-factor authentication to prevent unauthorized access. Additionally, the app’s design allows for bespoke partnerships to grow the user’s wealth and benefit the growth of Africa.

Our Outcome:

Through our expert mobile app development process and clear communication with the client, we were able to deliver a successful mobile app that met the client’s vision and exceeded their expectations. The app generated revenue quickly and had a viral growth of users. The app’s ability to solve the user’s problem effectively, made it a huge success in the market.
The client benefitted from working with an expert mobile app development company who understood their vision and could build a successful mobile app quickly. As a result, the client was able to provide a one-stop-shop for spending, investing, and interacting with Africa, with a focus on convenience and access to the African market.
