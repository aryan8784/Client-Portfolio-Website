Mobeen, a leading mobile app development company based in London, was approached by a client with a vision to launch a new crowdfunding platform. The client wanted to bring together early stage companies, influencers, and investors to create a community of owners and drive growth through pre-existing marketing channels. However, they faced several challenges in bringing their vision to life.

The Problem :

The client faced several challenges in bringing their vision to life. Firstly, the platform needed to be accessible to users across a range of devices, which meant creating a full stack mobile app that was optimized for both iOS and Android. Additionally, the team had to integrate a crowdfunding API that had been bespokely altered for the platform, working closely with the development team behind the API from the USA.

The Solution and Outcome :

Mobeen expertise in mobile app development and its unique 7-step process for building new products: visioning, scoping, designing, developing, testing, launching and iterating, proved to be the perfect solution for the client. The team employed best-in-class agile methodologies and used the latest code standards and principles to engineer a professional and reliable mobile app. They also successfully integrated a variety of protocols to ensure the platform passed FINRA inspection.
The outcome was a highly successful crowdfunding platform that has helped to unite early stage companies, influencers, and investors, driving growth and fostering a sense of community ownership.
